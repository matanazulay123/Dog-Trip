Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply?

+

To apply as a dog walker, click on the "Become a Dog Walker" link in the navigation menu. Fill out the application form with your details. Our team will review your application and get back to you.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

+

If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Log In" button and then select "Forgot Password." Enter the email address associated with your account, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password. If you don't receive the email, please check your spam folder or contact our support team.

How do I know that dog walkers are reliable?

+

We take the reliability of our dog walkers very seriously. All our dog walkers undergo a thorough background check and vetting process. Additionally, we have a rating system where you can see reviews from other pet owners. We also offer a satisfaction guarantee and 24/7 support to ensure the best experience for you and your furry friend.

What services do you offer?

+

We offer a range of pet care services to meet your needs:

  • Dog Walking: Scheduled walks tailored to your dog's energy level and preferences.
  • Dog Sitting: In-home pet sitting for when you're away.
All our services are customizable to fit your pet's specific needs and your schedule.
