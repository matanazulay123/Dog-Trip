About Us

Dog Trip is a platform dedicated to connecting dog lovers with responsible and experienced dog walkers and sitters. We believe that every dog deserves the best care and attention, and we strive to provide a safe and reliable service for both dog owners and walkers.

Our Mission

To make dog care convenient and accessible for everyone, ensuring that all dogs receive the love, attention, and exercise they need, while providing a rewarding opportunity for dog walkers and sitters.
